Art director - Experiential & graphic designer - Content creator -

I'm Anne, a creative spirit, born in France and currently living in the beautiful landscapes of Canada. My passion lies in creating and traveling the world, capturing moments through my lens that inspire, and immersing myself in extraordinary adventures all with a mindful touch, emphasizing connection and emotions.

Devoting myself entirely to creation, I've collaborated extensively with industry leaders like The National Geographic, L’Oréal, Mercedes-Benz, and numerous others to imagine memorable experiences, emotional content, and beautiful designs. My journey is an ongoing quest to extract the fullest potential from my own life, with a parallel commitment to inspire others to embrace intentional and courageous living.

What i offer

As a versatile creative specializing in experiential design, I bring a myriad of methods and mediums to breathe life into your projects. From ensuring visual cohesion to crafting diverse designs and impactful content, I am committed to delivering beautiful creations and memorable experiences for your brand.

  • I specialize in crafting cohesive and memorable designs and experiences for brands. As a resource person for a team, I bring strategic direction and work closely with other talents to ensure a consistent and impactful presence across all mediums.

  • I weave creativity and strategy into every project and on many different platforms, delivering visually stunning solutions that make your brand stand out.

  • I craft engaging narratives, eye-catching visuals, and impactful strategies to enhance your online presence and captivate your audience.

  • Transforming ideas into unforgettable experiences, I specialize in interactive and event design. From concept to execution, I create immersive environments that engage and captivate, ensuring your events leave a lasting impression.

  • Capturing moments that resonate, I specialize in delivering stunning visual narratives through photography. From compelling portraits to dynamic events, I bring your stories to life with creativity and precision.

  • I'm always up for new creative challenges, so if you have any particular ideas I could help you with, Please surprise me!
